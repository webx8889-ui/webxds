Transforming the Strength of Metal into the Language of Design

About Mecon

Based in Jamnagar, the Brass City of India, Mecon shapes metal into meaning. Every product they craft begins as molten brass and transforms into a symbol of precision and trust. Our goal was to reflect this journey through design, capturing the golden strength of brass, the brilliance of craftsmanship, and the timelessness of engineering excellence.

Industries

Export Manufacturing

Year

2025

Services

Branding | Logo Design

The Challenge

  • Industrial Identity Gap: The existing brand lacked a modern visual language that reflected its high-precision engineering on an international scale
  • Narrative Disconnect: Difficulty in communicating the craftsmanship journey from raw, molten brass to finished, high-trust industrial components
  • Market Positioning: A need to transition the visual identity from a traditional local manufacturer to a globally recognized industrial leader

The Solution

  • Golden Standard Strategy: Developed a visual narrative that celebrates the physical strength and brilliance of professional brass manufacturing
  • Transformative Logo Design: Crafted a logo representing structure and shine, mirroring the process of turning molten metal into finished parts
  • Precision-Led Visuals: Established a bold, industrial aesthetic that communicates reliability and engineering excellence to global export partners

Visual Identity

A framework designed to bridge the gap between heavy industry and modern elegance

Logo Concept

Inspired by Mecon's core process, from molten brass to finished components, the logo visually represents structure, shine, and transformation. It serves as a modern emblem of brass manufacturing excellence.

Colour Palette

We chose deep, industrial charcoals paired with rich metallic gold and brass tones. This palette conveys a sense of professional reliability and high-end value, perfectly mirroring the raw materials used in their export manufacturing.
