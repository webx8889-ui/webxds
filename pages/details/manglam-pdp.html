About Manglam Consultancy

Manglam Consultancy is a professionally managed team specializing in Tax and Loan Consultancy Services. They serve a wide range of manufacturers, traders, and service providers, offering expertise in accounting, taxation, and project finance. Our goal was to take their deep industry knowledge and translate it into a modern, trustworthy digital identity that simplifies complex financial services for their clients.

The Challenge

  • Outdated Brand Identity: An old-fashioned visual presence that did not reflect their expertise in modern financial solutions
  • Complex Service Presentation: Difficulty in organizing a wide range of services, such as NRI Advisory and Loan Consultancy, into a clear and cohesive format
  • Limited Digital Presence: A lack of a professional digital identity to communicate reliability to a growing modern client base
  • Low Brand Recall: A generic design style that failed to differentiate Manglam from competitors in the consultancy sector

The Solution

  • Modern Visual Identity: Created a fresh logo symbolizing financial stability and growth to align with Manglam's core values
  • Structured Communication: Reorganized their messaging to make complex services like Business Formation and Taxation more accessible
  • Digital-Ready System: Designed flexible brand elements that perform seamlessly across websites, social media, and mobile apps
  • Trust-Oriented Branding: Used professional colors and clean typography to reflect the confidence and transparency essential to financial success
