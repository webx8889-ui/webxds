About Arogya Bharat

Arogya Bharat is a leading provider of professional healthcare instruments, from Spirometer machines to pediatric mobility solutions. We transformed their digital storefront into a high-performance platform that reflects their commitment to excellence. By simplifying complex medical data and purchasing paths, we created a reliable bridge between advanced technology and the healthcare professionals who need it.

Industries

Services

The Challenge

  • Navigation Friction: Users struggled to find specific medical equipment due to poor categorization
  • Information Gap: Insufficient product data and low-quality imagery led to buyer hesitation
  • Checkout Complexity: A multi-step, unclear checkout process caused high cart abandonment
  • Trust Deficit: A lack of visible certifications and reviews affected professional confidence

The Solution

  • Advanced Search: Implemented a well-structured menu and filters for effortless product discovery
  • Data-Rich Pages: Added detailed specifications and high-quality visuals for informed decision-making
  • Seamless Checkout: Simplified the payment journey with clear shipping policies and guest options
  • Credential Focus: Integrated testimonials and certifications to establish industry authority
